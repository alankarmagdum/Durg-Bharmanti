About Devkund Waterfall Trek : 

  Embark on this trekking expedition to the Devkund waterfall, known as the 'Bathing Pond of Gods'. Located near Bhira Village, Devkund Waterfall is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in India. Witness the amalgamation of three waterfalls, where you can witness the origin of the Kundalika River after trekking for 3 hours. Witness the beauty of the forests around the waterfall, and complete the trek with an experienced trek leader. You will also be able to see the beautiful Bhira dam and Tamhini Ghat during this trek.

  • Get picked up from the above-mentioned pickup points by private non-AC bus.
  • The duration of the Devkund Waterfall trek is 1 day, where the journey towards the base village (Bhira village) takes approx 5 hours & the expedition to and from the Devkund waterfall takes 3 -4 hours (1.5 hours each way).
  • Reach the Devkund waterfall trek start point and enjoy a filling veg breakfast at the base village.
  • Cover the Devkund trek distance of 5-6 km and explore the beauty of mountains, valleys, lush greenery, dense jungles, scenic hamlets, and glistening waters.
  • The trek route comprises plain trails in the majority of the portions, with some medium ascent of 300 ft at the end of the trail.
  • Enjoy the beautiful view of the Bhira dam and Tamhini Ghat during this trek.
  • Descend down to the base village to grab a mouth-watering veg lunch after a tiresome day.

How to Reach : 

  • Devkund waterfall Trek is about 170 km from Mumbai and 110 km from Pune.
  • The package offers comfortable pick-up & drop-off service till the base village- Bhira, which can be availed on the selection of particular package options.
  • On choosing the package without transportation, you can reach the base point - Bhira village, which is about 7 hours distance from Mumbai and 5 hours distance from Pune. It can be easily accessed through Mumbai Pune Expressway.

Note  :
